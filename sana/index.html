What I Do

I am MS.SANA MATEEN, an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science at Methodist College Of Engineering And Technology, with over five years of teaching and research experience. I hold a Master's Degree in Computer Science from the Shadan Women's College Of Engineering And Technology, where my dissertation focused on enhancing skills in Ethical Hacking and Cybersecurity. I have had the privilege of teaching courses such as Artificial Intelligence,Computer Networks, Java Programming, Data Structures, Cyber Security, OOP using C++ consistently receiving high student evaluation scores.

My research interests include Cybersecurity and Ethical Hacking, and I have published several notable papers in esteemed journals and conferences. I actively participate in professional development by attending international conferences, completing specialized workshops, and earning certifications.
